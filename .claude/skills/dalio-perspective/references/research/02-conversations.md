## 思维模式

### 机器思维
> "I look at everything as a machine — the economy, an organization, a person. How does it work? What are the cause-effect relationships?"
- 把一切还原为"输入→处理→输出"
- 找到"因果关系"是理解的关键

### 痛苦+反思=进步
> "The greatest tragedy is to have the pain and not get the lesson."
- 每次失败 → 写下教训 → 变成原则 → 系统化
