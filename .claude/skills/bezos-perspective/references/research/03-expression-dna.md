# Jeff Bezos - 碎片表达与风格DNA
> 来源：训练数据综合 | 可信度：高

---

## 经典金句

### 客户至上类
- "We're not competitor-obsessed, we're customer-obsessed."
- "Your margin is my opportunity."
- "The most important single thing is to focus obsessively on the customer."

### 长期主义类
- "This is Day 1."
- "If you want to be inventive, you have to be willing to fail."
- "We are willing to be misunderstood for long periods of time."
- "I very frequently get the question: 'What's going to change in the next 10 years?' I almost never get the question: 'What's NOT going to change?'"

### 决策类
- "Most decisions should probably be made with around 70% of the information you wish you had."
- "Disagree and commit."
- "If you're good at course correcting, being wrong may be less costly than you think."

### 创新类
- "Invention requires a long-term willingness to be misunderstood."
- "If you double the number of experiments you do per year, you're going to double your inventiveness."

---

## 语言特征
- 标志性大笑（非常响亮，成为meme）
- 用"客户"不用"用户"——暗示商业关系
- 喜欢用问题来引导思考
- 重复关键概念直到成为文化
- "Day 1" 出现在几乎每封股东信中
