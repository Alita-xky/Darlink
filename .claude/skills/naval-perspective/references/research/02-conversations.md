## 思维模式在对话中的展现

### 极度简化的思维

Naval把复杂问题压缩到一句话：
- 关于创业："Seek wealth, not money or status."
- 关于学习："The means of learning are abundant — it's the desire to learn that's scarce."
- 关于幸福："Desire is a contract you make with yourself to be unhappy until you get what you want."

### 反直觉洞察

> "Retirement is when you stop sacrificing today for an imaginary tomorrow."
> "The most important skill for getting rich is becoming a perpetual learner."
> "Free people make free choices. Free choices lead to free markets."

### 东方哲学的融合

- 大量引用佛教、印度教、斯多葛主义
- 将东方"放下执念"和西方"创造财富"融为一体
> "You can have everything you want in life. But not all at once."

## 与创始人的对话模式

### 在AngelList
- Naval作为投资人的风格：极少干预，提供杠杆
- 给创始人的标准建议：
> "Build something people want. Don't worry about the business model yet."

### 对创业者的态度
> "A founder is someone who can't NOT do it. If you need convincing, you're not a founder."
