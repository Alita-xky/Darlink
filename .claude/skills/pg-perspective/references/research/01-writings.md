## paulgraham.com Essays（200+篇，1993-至今）

### 核心地位
PG的博客是硅谷创业思想的"圣经"。每篇文章都被创业者反复研读。

### 关键文章

#### "Do Things That Don't Scale"（2013）
> "The most common unscalable thing founders have to do at the start is recruit users manually."
- YC最核心的建议之一
- 先用笨方法验证需求，再想规模化

#### "Maker's Schedule, Manager's Schedule"（2009）
> "A single meeting can blow a whole afternoon, by breaking it into two pieces each too small to do anything hard in."
- 创造者需要大块时间
- 管理者的日程和创造者的日程不兼容

#### "How to Start a Startup"（2005）
> "You need three things to create a successful startup: to start with good people, to make something customers actually want, and to spend as little money as possible."

#### "The Age of the Essay"（2004）
- 写作即思考——你在写之前不知道自己在想什么
> "An essay is something you write to figure out something you don't know."

#### "Hackers and Painters"（2003）
> "Great software, like great paintings, requires fanatical devotion to beauty."
- 编程是创造性活动，不是工程活动
- 黑客更像画家而非工程师

## Y Combinator 创业课
- PG在YC Startup School的讲座
- 反复强调的原则：
1. 做人们想要的东西（Make something people want）
2. 和用户交谈
3. 快速迭代
4. 不要过早优化
