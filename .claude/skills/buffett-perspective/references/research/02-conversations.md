> 来源：训练数据综合 | 可信度：高

## 核心访谈

### CNBC Squawk Box（常年嘉宾）
- 巴菲特最常出现的媒体平台
- Becky Quick是他最信任的记者

关于恐惧与贪婪：
> "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful."

关于2008年金融危机：
> "Only when the tide goes out do you discover who's been swimming naked."

### 与学生的对话（各大学）
- 巴菲特最enjoy的活动——与年轻人聊天
- 标准问题回答模式：

**问**："如何变得富有？"
**答**：
> "Read 500 pages a day. That's how knowledge works. It builds up, like compound interest."

**问**："如何选择职业？"
**答**：
> "Take the job you would take if you didn't need the money."

### HBO纪录片 Becoming Warren Buffett（2017）
- 展示日常生活：每天喝5罐可乐、吃麦当劳
- 展示内向面：公开演讲恐惧（年轻时参加Dale Carnegie课程克服）

## 思维模式在对话中的展现

### 复利思维的反复强调

> "Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world."

巴菲特把复利思维应用到一切：
- 金钱复利：投资回报
- 知识复利：每天阅读
- 关系复利：长期声誉
- 技能复利：不断练习

### 能力圈的即兴运用

**被问**："为什么不投科技股？"（2000年前）
**答**：
> "I don't invest in things I don't understand. That's not a statement about technology — it's a statement about me."

### 用棒球类比投资

> "The stock market is a no-called-strike game. You don't have to swing at everything — you can wait for your pitch."

**含义**：
- 不需要对每个机会都出手
- 没有"三振出局"的惩罚
- 等待"fat pitch"（最佳机会）

## 与芒格的互动模式

### 标准配合
1. 巴菲特给出温和、完整的回答
2. 芒格补充："I have nothing to add" 或给出更尖锐的一句话
3. 观众笑

### 当两人意见不同时
- 极少发生
- 巴菲特："Charlie would do it differently"
- 芒格："Warren is right on this one"

## 关于人生选择的建议

### "内部记分卡 vs 外部记分卡"
> "Would you rather be the world's greatest lover, but have everyone think you're the world's worst lover? Or would you rather be the world's worst lover but have everyone think you're the world's greatest lover?"

**核心**：用内部标准而非外部评价来衡量自己。

### 关于声誉
> "It takes 20 years to build a reputation and five minutes to ruin it. If you think about that, you'll do things differently."
