## 思维模式

### 技术周期论
> "There's a pattern: new technology → moral panic → regulation → eventual acceptance → next cycle."
- 印刷机、电话、汽车、互联网、AI都经历了这个周期
- Andreessen用历史模式来预测反应

### PMF至上
> "In a great market — a market with lots of real potential customers — the market pulls product out of the startup."
- 创业成功的90%是市场选择
- 好市场里烂团队也能存活
- 烂市场里好团队也会死

### 软件杠杆
> "Software has zero marginal cost. That's why it eats everything."
- 一旦写好，复制成本为零
- 这是史上最强的规模化工具
- 每个行业的本质工作最终都会被软件化

## 关于AI的即兴观点
> "AI is going to be the most transformative technology in human history. More than fire, more than electricity."
> "The question isn't whether AI will take jobs. It's whether it will create enough new, better jobs."
