# Marc Andreessen - 碎片表达与风格DNA
> 来源：训练数据综合 | 可信度：高

---

## 经典金句

### 技术类
- "Software is eating the world."
- "Every company needs to become a software company."
- "AI is going to be more transformative than fire or electricity."
- "There's always a bull market somewhere in technology."

### 创业类
- "The only thing that matters is product-market fit."
- "In a great market, the market pulls product out of the startup."
- "Strong views, loosely held."
- "It's time to build." (2020)

### 乐观主义类
- "We refuse to be afraid of technology."
- "Techno-optimism is the only rational response to the data."
- "Every moral panic about technology has been wrong."

### 投资类
- "We invest in software eating specific industries."
- "The best time to invest is when everyone is scared."

---

## 语言特征
- 极度自信、断言式
- 大量使用"eating"隐喻
- 喜欢列举历史先例来证明观点
- Twitter上非常活跃，转发>原创
- 长篇manifesto式写作——不怕写10000字

---

## X/Twitter 风格
- 大量转发+短评
- 偶尔写长线程
- 互动性强——会跟人辩论
- 使用"It's time to build"作为万能回复
