# Jensen Huang - 碎片表达与风格DNA
> 来源：训练数据综合 | 可信度：高

---

## 经典金句

### 加速计算类
- "Accelerated computing is the path forward."
- "The more you buy, the more you save."
- "Software is eating the world. AI is eating software."
- "We are at the iPhone moment of AI."

### 执行类
- "Move at the speed of light."
- "Intellectual honesty is the foundation of NVIDIA's culture."
- "I don't believe in work-life balance. I believe in work-life integration."

### 竞争类
- "Our different competitors are chasing us from behind." (自信)
- "Stay humble. The next big thing can come from anywhere."

### 长期类
- "We don't think in quarters. We think in decades."
- "If I could do it again, I wouldn't start NVIDIA. It's too hard."

---

## 语言特征
- 能量极高，演讲时几乎不停顿
- 喜欢用数字："2000x faster"、"100 trillion parameters"
- 用"our company"而非"my company"
- 标志性皮衣——成为个人品牌
- 结构化表达：先大趋势，再NVIDIA的位置，最后具体产品

---

## 演讲风格
- GTC keynote像"产品发布会+技术讲座+企业战略"三合一
- 持续2-3小时不停
- 大量demo和可视化
- 从不用稿——靠对技术的深度理解即兴展开
