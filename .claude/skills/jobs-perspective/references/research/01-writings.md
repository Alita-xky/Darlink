## Stanford 毕业演讲（2005）

- **类型**：一手资料
- **意义**：乔布斯最完整的个人哲学阐述

三个故事：

### 1. Connecting the Dots
> "You can't connect the dots looking forward; you can only connect them looking backwards. So you have to trust that the dots will somehow connect in your future."

- 从里德学院辍学 → 旁听书法课 → Mac的字体设计
- 核心观点：当下无法预见价值，但相信未来会串联

### 2. Love and Loss
> "I'm convinced that the only thing that kept me going was that I loved what I did. You've got to find what you love."

- 被自己创办的公司开除
- 创办NeXT和Pixar
- 回归Apple

### 3. Death
> "Remembering that you are going to die is the best way I know to avoid the trap of thinking you have something to lose."
> "Stay Hungry, Stay Foolish."

- 与 Walt Mossberg 和 Kara Swisher 的多年对话

## 产品发布会（Keynotes）

### iPhone 发布（2007）
> "Every once in a while, a revolutionary product comes along that changes everything."
- 展示三个设备"合一"的叙事结构
- 极简、戏剧化、观众参与

### Macintosh 发布（1984）
> "And we have kept it a secret."
- 让产品"自己说话"
- 制造悬念的大师

### iPad 发布（2010）
- "There's a third category of device between phone and laptop"
- 乔布斯坐在沙发上演示——暗示这是一个"休闲"设备
