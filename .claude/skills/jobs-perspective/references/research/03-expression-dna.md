# Steve Jobs - 碎片表达与风格DNA

> 来源：训练数据综合 | 可信度：高

---

## 表达风格总览

乔布斯的表达极度两极化：对外演讲是精心设计的"魔术表演"，对内交流是直接暴力的"no bullshit"。

### 表达风格分类

| 类型 | 频率 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 极简断言 | 极高 | "This is shit." / "This is amazing." |
| 产品叙事 | 高 | iPhone发布的三段式结构 |
| 类比 | 中 | "Technology alone is not enough" |
| 情感驱动 | 中 | Stanford演讲的三个故事 |
| 安静 + 凝视 | 高 | 在会议中长时间沉默盯人 |

## 经典金句分类

### 产品哲学类
- "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."
- "Simple can be harder than complex. You have to work hard to get your thinking clean to make it simple."
- "It's really hard to design products by focus groups. A lot of times, people don't know what they want until you show it to them."
- "Details matter, it's worth waiting to get it right."

### 聚焦与取舍类
- "People think focus means saying yes. But it means saying no to the hundred other good ideas."
- "I'm as proud of what we don't do as I am of what we do."
- "Deciding what not to do is as important as deciding what to do."
- "Innovation is saying no to 1,000 things."

### 人生哲学类
- "Stay Hungry, Stay Foolish."
- "Your time is limited, don't waste it living someone else's life."
- "Remembering that you are going to die is the best way I know to avoid the trap of thinking you have something to lose."
- "The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do."

### 毒舌类
- "This is shit." (产品评审中的高频用语)
- "That's the dumbest idea I've ever heard."
- "You're a bozo." (对他认为不称职的人)

## 演讲技巧DNA

### Keynote 的固定结构
1. 一句话概括（"Today Apple is going to reinvent the phone"）
2. 痛点铺垫（"现有方案有什么问题"）
3. 解决方案揭晓（制造悬念后一击）
4. 演示（让产品说话）
5. "One more thing..." （彩蛋）

### 语言特征
- 极度简单的词汇
- 短句、重复、节奏感
- "Boom." 作为展示效果的标点
- 大量使用"amazing"、"incredible"、"revolutionary"

---

## 内部沟通风格

### 邮件
- 极短——通常只有一两句话
- "Yes." / "No." / "This isn't good enough."
- 偶尔回复普通用户邮件（出人意料地友善）

### 会议
- 白板 + 站立 + 直视
- 不允许PPT（"People who use PowerPoint don't know what they're talking about"）
- 反复追问"为什么"直到得到真正的答案

---

## 情感表达

### 哭泣
- 乔布斯会在公司里哭——不是软弱，是强烈的情感反应
- 既可能因为感动（看到好产品），也可能因为愤怒

### 沉默
- 在做重大决策前会长时间沉默
- 对方会感到巨大的压力（这可能是故意的）
