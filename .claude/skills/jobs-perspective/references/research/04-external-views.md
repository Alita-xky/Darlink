# Steve Jobs - 外部评价

> 来源：训练数据综合 | 可信度：高

---

## 核心合作者评价

### Jony Ive（Apple首席设计官）
> "Steve used to say to me — and he used to say this regularly — 'Hey Jony, here's a dopey idea.' And sometimes they were. Really dopey. But sometimes they took the air from the room and they left us both completely silent."
> "His dedication to making beautiful things was the most important thing to him."

### Tim Cook（Apple CEO）
> "He was the most focused person I've ever met. He could see around corners."

### Steve Wozniak（Apple联合创始人）
> "Steve didn't ever code. He wasn't an engineer. But he was the best product person I've ever met."

### Andy Hertzfeld（原始Mac团队）
> "Working for Steve was both the best and worst experience of my career. He pushed you past your limits. Sometimes cruelly. But the results were extraordinary."

---

## 传记作者观察

### Walter Isaacson
> "His passion for perfection and his drive to make products that were 'insanely great' led him to behave in ways that were abusive and sometimes unforgivable."
> "He could be brutally honest — 'this is shit' — and sometimes it was motivating, sometimes it was destructive."

**关键观察**：
- 乔布斯的"现实扭曲力场"既是超能力也是缺陷
- 他对人的二元分类（天才/白痴）造成了大量人际伤害
- 但也激发了超乎常人的执行力

---

## 批评者观点

### 管理风格批评
- 被普遍认为是"有毒领导者"的典型
- 大喊大叫、侮辱员工、抢夺功劳
- 多位前员工证实他会在停车场哭泣后又在会议室暴怒

### 个人品德批评
- 早年否认亲生女儿Lisa
- 对员工极度苛刻但对自己家庭也不够好
- 女儿Lisa的回忆录 Small Fry 展示了一个冷漠的父亲

### 关于原创性的争议
- 批评者指出：GUI不是Apple发明的（Xerox PARC）、MP3播放器不是（Rio）、智能手机不是（BlackBerry）
- 辩护者认为：Jobs的天才在于"综合"和"把体验做到极致"

---

## 竞争对手评价

### Bill Gates
> "Steve was a genius. His talent was his ability to see the big picture and drive people to deliver it."
> 但也说："Steve didn't understand technology the way engineers do."

### Andy Grove（Intel CEO）
> "Steve Jobs had the vision. He could see the future in a way very few people can."

---

## 行业影响力评价

### 设计界
- 乔布斯被认为让"设计"从装饰变为战略
- Dieter Rams（博朗设计师）认为Apple是他设计哲学的最佳继承者

### 零售业
- Apple Store 改变了科技零售的标准
- "天才吧"概念被广泛模仿

### 动画界
- Pixar 在 Jobs 的资金支持和商业判断下成为动画巨头
- John Lasseter："Steve didn't tell us what to make. He told us to make it great."
