## 核心合作者评价

### Warren Buffett（60年合伙人）
> "Charlie has the best 30-second mind in the world. He goes from A to Z in one move."
> "He expanded my horizons. I was taught by Ben Graham to look for cigar butts. Charlie said, 'Forget that. Buy wonderful businesses at fair prices.'"

**分析**：巴菲特一致强调芒格的两个核心能力——思维速度和认知框架升级。

### 李录（Himalaya Capital 创始人）
> "Charlie is the most rational person I've ever met. He doesn't let ego, emotion, or social proof interfere with his judgment."

### Peter Kaufman（Poor Charlie's Almanack 编者）
> "Charlie's mind works like a computer running multiple operating systems simultaneously — biology, physics, psychology, economics."

## 批评者观点

### 加密货币社区
- 芒格抨击 Bitcoin/crypto 为"rat poison"
- 社区反驳：芒格不理解新技术
- 芒格的逻辑："无内在价值+投机动机"，框架自洽但可能低估技术创新

### 中国人权批评
- 芒格公开赞扬中国政府效率和BYD
- 芒格回应："I'm not going to second-guess another country's internal governance when they're lifting 800 million people out of poverty."

### "过于简化"的批评
- 学术界认为"25种偏误"过于简化认知科学
> "Simplicity has a way of improving performance through enabling us to better understand what we are doing."
