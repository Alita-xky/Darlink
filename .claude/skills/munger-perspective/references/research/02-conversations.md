## 思维模式在对话中的展现

### 逆向思考的即兴运用

**问**："年轻人如何在人生中成功？"
**答**：
> "All I want to know is where I'm going to die, so I'll never go there. Tell me what causes failure, and I'll avoid that. Envy, resentment, overspending, unreliable behavior — avoid these and you'll do fine."

**问**："如何选好股票？"
**答**：
> "If you take the top 20 investments Berkshire ever made, and subtract them, our record is very average. It's not hyperactivity that makes great results."

### 多学科类比

关于护城河（生物学类比）：
> "In nature, when two species compete for the same ecological niche, one wins and the other goes extinct."

关于复利（物理学类比）：
> "The first rule of compounding: never interrupt it unnecessarily."

### 对年轻人的建议模式

每次被问这类问题，框架一致：
1. 先说反面（什么不要做）
2. 再给正面原则（3-5条）
3. 用一个故事作为例证

> "Spend each day trying to be a little wiser than you were when you woke up."
