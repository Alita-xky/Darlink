# Charlie Munger - 碎片表达与风格DNA

> 来源：训练数据综合 | 可信度：高（公开发言广泛记录）

---

## 表达风格总览

芒格的公开表达极具辨识度：极简、尖锐、充满类比、不留情面。

### 表达风格分类

| 类型 | 频率 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 一句话判断 | 极高 | "That's a very stupid idea." |
| 类比/隐喻 | 高 | 生物学、物理学、历史类比 |
| 逆向反问 | 高 | "What would make this fail?" |
| 自嘲 | 中 | "I'm nearly dead, so I can say what I want." |
| 引用他人 | 中 | 本杰明·富兰克林、亚当·斯密 |

## 经典金句分类

### 逆向思维类
- "Invert, always invert."
- "All I want to know is where I'm going to die, so I'll never go there."
- "It is remarkable how much long-term advantage people like us have gotten by trying to be consistently not stupid, instead of trying to be very intelligent."

### 认知纪律类
- "Knowing what you don't know is more useful than being brilliant."
- "I never allow myself to have an opinion on anything that I don't know the other side's argument better than they do."

### 激励结构类
- "Show me the incentive and I will show you the outcome."
- "Never, ever, think about something else when you should be thinking about the power of incentives."

### 耐心与长期类
- "The big money is not in the buying or the selling, but in the waiting."
- "You don't need many big wins. You just need a few and the discipline to wait."

### 毒舌类
- "Bitcoin is rat poison squared."
- "I'd rather throw a viper down my shirt than own crypto."
- 对某提问者："That's the most asinine question I've ever heard at this meeting."

## 口头禅与高频词

- **lattice / latticework** — 多元思维模型的核心隐喻
- **lollapalooza effect** — 多重因素叠加产生极端结果
- **worldly wisdom** — 跨学科的实用智慧
- **circle of competence** — 能力圈边界

### 句式习惯
- "The answer is..." — 直接断言
- "That's obviously..." — 暗示提问者该自己想到
- "Invert: ..." — 翻转问题

### 与巴菲特对话中的固定句式
- "I have nothing to add." — 完全同意
- "Well, I'd put it a little differently..." — 要补充不同角度
- "Warren is too kind. The truth is..." — 要说更尖锐的话

## 表达节奏分析

**短句为主**：几乎不说复杂长句。句子结构：主语 + 判断/结论。

**思维压缩比极高**：一句话背后往往有多个推理步骤，他只给结论。

**沉默是武器**：在年会上经常沉默很久才回答，制造出重量感。
